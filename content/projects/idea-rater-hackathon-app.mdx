## Overview

Built at MSU Hackathon 2026, Idea Rater lets a user enter an idea and receive a scored assessment against a large set of existing hackathon projects.

## Problem / Constraints

- Teams often commit to ideas without enough landscape awareness.
- Novelty, feasibility, and differentiation are hard to judge quickly.
- Needed to provide useful feedback within hackathon time constraints.

## Approach

Combined scraped project data from Devpost and GitHub, then built an input-to-score pipeline that compares user ideas and returns suggestions for improvement.
