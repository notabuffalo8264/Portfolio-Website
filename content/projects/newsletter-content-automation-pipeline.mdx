## Overview

Designed a personal content pipeline that runs daily and compiles relevant news into a structured email digest sent from my Gmail account to myself.

## Problem / Constraints

- Too many sources to manually review each day.
- Needed filtering that matched my actual interests, not generic popularity.
- Required a fully automated and reliable daily delivery workflow.

## Approach

Built an n8n flow that:

- ingests RSS feeds from selected outlets
- evaluates and ranks items by value/relevance
- organizes and formats digest content
- sends final output through Gmail on a recurring trigger
