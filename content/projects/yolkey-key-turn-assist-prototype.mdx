## Overview

Yolkey was created during a 24-hour design sprint at MSU. The goal was to make key turning easier for users with limited wrist mobility, especially elderly users, without relying on electronics.

## Problem / Constraints

- Very limited project window (24 hours from concept to presentation)
- Needed a clear accessibility benefit with a straightforward user interaction
- Solution had to remain fully mechanical and low complexity

## Approach

Designed a gear-based torque manipulation concept so users could achieve the same key-turn result with lower required wrist effort.

My contribution focused on creating the initial CAD geometry and defining the base mechanism architecture quickly enough for team iteration and final presentation.
