Developed an assistive mechanism concept to improve accessibility for key-based interfaces.

## Problem / Constraints

- Durability targets under repeated actuation
- Ergonomic usability for broad user range

## Approach

Evaluated linkage concepts, prototyped alternatives, and tested torque profiles under repeat cycles.
