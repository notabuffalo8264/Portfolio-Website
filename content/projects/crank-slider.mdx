## Overview

This ME470 project focused on developing a crank-slider paperweight from concept through fabrication and communication deliverables.

## Problem / Constraints

- Needed a mechanically sound design that could be manufactured within class constraints.
- Required material/process decisions balancing precision, cost, and timeline.
- Final output included both physical hardware and formal reporting/presentation.

## Approach

Developed a mixed-manufacturing strategy: primarily metal components for structure and mechanism quality, plus 3D-printed parts where appropriate for geometry and iteration speed.
